Our Story

About OTC by 4 Lal Ghat

Nestled in the heart of Old City, Silawatwari, Udaipur, OTC by 4 Lal Ghat is a boutique heritage-style hotel that blends modern comfort with timeless Rajasthani charm. Located at 4, Lal Ghat Road, our property offers breathtaking rooftop views of Lake Pichola and is just steps away from iconic landmarks like Jagdish Temple, Bagore Ki Haveli, and Gangaur Ghat.

With a 4.4-star rating from 481+ reviews, guests love our beautifully designed rooms, rooftop restaurant serving delicious food, and the warm hospitality of our polite and friendly staff. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway or a cultural exploration, OTC by 4 Lal Ghat is your perfect Udaipur base.
